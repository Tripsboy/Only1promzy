About

Promzy is a fast-rising music artist. Born Abioye Hammed Adeyemi and a student of LAUTECH, Promzy became involved with music while growing up in the trenches of Iwo Road, Ibadan and performing in his secondary school socials. He plays Afrobeat, Afrofusion with a feel of RnB, Dancehall and he is also versatile across other genres of music.

Man for the ladies

Promzy has become one of the most listened to artists on the airwaves in Ibadan, and one could easily feel his vibes in his songs, which are spot-on sing-along songs that most people like. No wonder, he was billed to perform in major rave events like Cruzer Reloaded Concert, Brown Roof Party, Ibadan Music Festival, Party with Pivotal, UnderG Block Party (LAUTECH) and Wale Waves and Friends. With the competitive music industry we have right now, Promzy is blazing the trail and making sense of the city of Ibadan in the mainstream.
